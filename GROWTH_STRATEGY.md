### 2. Reddit Strategy
- **Subreddits to target**:
  - `/r/collectibles` - Share your collection management features
  - `/r/comicbooks` - Highlight comic collection tracking
  - `/r/vinyl` - Showcase vinyl record collection features
  - `/r/gamecollecting` - Video game collection tracking
  - `/r/boardgames` - Board game collection management
  - `/r/SideProject` - Share your journey building Colletro
  - `/r/webdev` - Technical showcase (if you have interesting tech)
- **Strategy**: 
  - Don't just promote - provide value (tips, guides, insights)
  - Share your story and journey
  - Engage authentically with the community

### 3. Social Media Presence
- **Twitter/X**: 
  - Share collection tips, features, user stories
  - Use hashtags: #collectibles #collectionmanagement #webapp
  - Engage with collectors and creators
- **Instagram**: 
  - Visual showcase of collections
  - User-generated content
  - Behind-the-scenes of building the app
- **TikTok/YouTube Shorts**:
  - Quick collection organization tips
  - "Before/After" collection organization videos
  - Feature highlights in 30-60 second videos

### 4. Content Marketing
- **Blog/Articles** (add to your site):
  - "How to Organize Your Collection: A Complete Guide"
  - "10 Collectibles Worth Tracking in 2024"
  - "The Psychology of Collecting: Why We Collect"
  - "Digital vs Physical Collection Tracking"
- **SEO Benefits**: Drive organic traffic, establish authority

### 5. Referral Program
- **Implementation**: 
  - Add "Invite Friends" feature
  - Give badges/achievements for referrals
  - Offer premium features or early access for referrers
  - Track referrals in user profiles
